Michelle Ngo is a director, producer, and writer based in New York City, with over twenty years in media production. She began her career writing features for a bilingual magazine in Tokyo, then transitioned to documentary work in New York.

Her recent producing credits include HBO's *Billy Joel: And So It Goes*, HBO's *The Janes*, Hulu's *The Lady Bird Diaries*, and PBS's Peabody-winning *Rising Against Asian Hate: One Day in March*. She also produced *PBS American Portrait*, an interactive storytelling series amplifying personal stories of contemporary America.

Michelle is a member of the Archival Producers Alliance and currently serves as archival producer on the documentary *The Inquisitor*.
